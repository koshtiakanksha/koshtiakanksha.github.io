About

I am Akanksha. Here is how it all came together.

Math was my first love. I never had to study it twice. Puzzles, quizzes, sudoku — they just made sense. That early joy of solving things never really left me. When I discovered statistics, it felt like math with purpose. I followed that path through undergrad and a master's, learning how things work and why they matter.

Somewhere along the way, I met code. What started as a course requirement quickly became something more. I saw how a few lines could replace hours of manual effort and scale beyond anything I could do by hand. That changed everything. I began building small tools, automating tasks, and solving problems I cared about. The blend of statistics and technology gave me the clarity and confidence to take on real challenges.

As I explored different fields, I knew I wanted to work on ideas that help businesses grow and improve how people work. That led me to Information Systems, where I could bring together strategy, systems, and data to build something useful. It also led me to one of my most meaningful projects so far — Accessible AI.

The first time I pitched that venture in front of a panel, I felt everything I had learned come together. It was not just about models or numbers anymore. It was about people, communication, and standing behind a vision. That moment taught me that building solutions is not just technical. It also takes clarity, direction, and the ability to bring others along.

Pursuing an MBA is my way of building those skills the same way I once built logic and code. And somehow, it still feels like solving a puzzle. Different pieces, new challenges, but the same joy I felt when I first opened a math book. The circle feels complete. And I am just getting started.

Accessible AI: Real-Time Speech, Text, and Sign Language Bridge

Built a deep learning-powered accessibility tool combining speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and sign language generation using NLP and computer vision. Enabled real-time multimodal translation through a browser-based interface to assist people with speech and hearing disabilities.


Emergency Management App: Real-Time Response Coordination

Designed a full-stack emergency response platform using Streamlit, FastAPI, and PostgreSQL with live geospatial tracking via Folium. Integrated OpenAI chatbot for incident classification and implemented secure, scalable backend features including role-based access and rate limiting.


Bringing Anime to Life: Advanced Face Generation Using DCGAN

Developed a DCGAN model to generate high-quality anime faces with 94.34% real image detection accuracy. Optimized architecture using leaky ReLU and quantization to enhance performance and training stability.


Reading Emotions: Deep Learning-Based Facial Expression Recognition

Built a CNN-based emotion detection model using the FER2013 dataset. Applied image normalization, data augmentation, and training visualizations. Achieved 63% accuracy with in-depth evaluation of misclassified samples and model reliability.


Breathing Better: Time Series Analysis of Air Quality Index

Analyzed pollution data from the Central Pollution Control Board and used ARIMA models for AQI forecasting. Derived data-driven recommendations to reduce AQI by 5%, supported by correlation analysis and environmental research review.


Customer Retention Analytics: Deep Learning for Churn Prediction

Designed a deep learning model using Keras to predict telecom customer churn. Achieved 78% accuracy after thorough data preprocessing and model tuning. Results helped inform targeted retention strategies.


Decoding Genetic Literature: NLP-Based QTL Analysis

Applied NLP techniques to analyze genetic research abstracts for QTL relevance. Built a BERT classifier with 75% accuracy and trained Word2Vec models to map terminology relationships. Visualized trends using TF-IDF and word clouds to uncover key traits.
